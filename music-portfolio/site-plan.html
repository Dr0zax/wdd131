Overview

Purpose

this site will be a portfolio for the music I write. It will include links to the place where I pubish my music, a contact form, and links to my social media. The child page will show what gear I use with information about each item

Audience

My audience will be those interested in my music and the tools I use. It is also for those who want to reach out to me

Dynamic elements

I will be using a javascript libary called GSAP to provide animations and make the site more interactive. I will have a data file with information about my gear and will dynamically add html to the page with the information.

Content

Home page

I'm Drozax. I'm a music producer and web developer. I write electronic music, specifically synthwave. I have been a producer since I was 15 and a musician since I was 10. My mother started me on piano lessons, and I quickly fell in love with it and music. I started experimenting with writing my own songs and published my first one in 2019 using Garageband on my iPad. Not long after, I downloaded the trial version of FL Studios and convinced my parents to buy me the full version. I would intermittently write and publish songs on my YouTube channel and SoundCloud until I started college. After my first semester, I made my own Bandcamp account and started my debut album, which I finished 6 months later on May 27th, 2024, with an EP shortly after.

I will be using my artist logo as the image in the home page

My Gear

The software I use to write my music is FL Studios 24 Producers Edition, Arturia V Collection 9, and Serum. The Hardware I use to write my music is a Korg Minilogue XD, a Yamaha ModX 7, an M-Audio Air 192 | 4, and an Epiphone Les Paul II electric guitar.

I will have a json file that contains the images for the gear I use and will dynamicaly add them to the page along side the information with JS
